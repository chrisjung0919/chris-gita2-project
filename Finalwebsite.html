How to Play

.


This game uses a lot of strategies and usually difficult to play
Each game takes around 3 minutes
If player wins it will give around 30 trophies
If player loses it will remove around 30 trophies
If the game is tied there will be no changes in trophies


How to win: player has to destroy king tower or more princess tower to win the game
Player has to place their cards and battle with the opponent
These are the cards that player has to use and shows the cost of elixirs
Each player has 10 elixirs and has to use it wisely to win the game
2 Princess Towers and 1 King Tower


There are many different and new strategies that player can use to win the battle
The key to win is using exlixirs wisely and upgrade cards to make the deck better
Level of cards are very crucial factor that can help player to win easily
Therefore, this game requires payment to help player win and increase their level



























Cards

.


This page describes many different types of cards
It will help people to learn which card is better or more useful in the deck
Player needs to have 8 cards in each deck
The better combination of cards will have a higher chance to win


Rarity:
Legendary
Epic
Rare
Common

Card Level:
1 - 14
Higher level cards are stronger

Total 106 cards
Unlock cards by opening chests

Deploying cards costs Elixir, which is gradually gained during battle
Cards are primarily obtained through buying them with Gold or Gems in the Shop, or through finding them in Chests

Cards can also be received through trading or through clan members fulfilling requests.
























Battle/Party

.


Different types of game modes and other ways to play the game
These modes help players to play casually rather than always playing ranked matches
Casual game modes does not affect trophies
Examples: Tournament, 2v2, Friendly Battle, Touchdown, Elixir Challenges, and Heist


Party can be played with a random teammate, a Clanmate or a Friend
The Party Button is your access to a more casual gaming experience compared to the rest of the game
Any match played from the Party Button will give you: Crowns. Victory Gold
ou can either play with a random teammate, a friend, or your clanmates


Click on the 'Party! ' button in the middle of the Battle tab
Select the game mode that you wish to play
Click on the 'Play with Friend' button and invite the respective player
The invited player will then join the game queue as soon as they accept the invitation



























Levels/Arenas

.


Levels of King’s Tower and different types of arenas
King’s Tower level: 1-14
The higher King’s Tower level makes towers stronger
Also unlocks new features
Getting more trophies will reach into a higher arena
Higher arena is more competitive and has more difficulties
Arenas: 1 - 15


Each Arena, besides the Legendary Arena, will unlock new rewards, like Chests
Players with a higher Arena level will contain more Gold and Cards
Once a player advances to a higher Arena, they cannot be demoted to a lower Arena
The Legendary Arena can always change styles every season


The amount of Experience points a player has determines their King Level
King Level indicates the level of the player's Crown Towers
Experience cannot be gained by simply battling
Experience is gained by upgrading cards and donating cards to clanmates
Earn more XP from upgrading higher level cards
The amount that you earn depends on the level of the card you upgrade






















Clan

.


Clan and members in clash royale
Each clan has maximum of 50 members
Clan War - Clans compete against other Clans for rewards and Clan Trophies
Player can donate their cards to earn XP and Star Points
Clan members can request cards every 7 hours


Clanmates can donate cards to each other and work together to fight Clan Wars
Once the player has completed the tutorial, they are allowed to join or create a Clan
However, the player must be at least King Level 3 to request cards
The player can initiate Friendly Battles in a Clan
Clans can have up to 50 members


To create a brand new clan, the player has to complete the tutorial
They must also pay 1,000 Gold to create a clan
Once the clan is created, the creator of the clan automatically becomes the leader of the clan
